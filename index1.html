About ICICCS & Call for Special Sessions

The IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Complex Systems (ICICCS 2026) will be held in Tabarka, Tunisia, from March 26–29, 2026. We invite proposals for Special Sessions that highlight emerging topics, interdisciplinary challenges, or niche areas complementing the main tracks of the conference.

Special Sessions are a unique opportunity to gather researchers and practitioners to discuss focused research themes and foster collaboration.

Special Session: AI for Industry – Call for Papers

This Special Session aims to explore the impact and applications of Artificial Intelligence in industrial contexts. We invite submissions that address innovative uses of AI in areas such as agriculture, disease detection, explainability, and beyond. Our goal is to bridge research and practice, fostering discussions on AI-driven transformation in various industries.

Topics of Interest

We welcome contributions on topics including, but not limited to:

  • AI for agriculture and smart farming
  • Disease detection and healthcare applications
  • Explainable and trustworthy AI in industry
  • Emerging paradigms in AI and complex systems
  • AI for law, economics, and sustainable development
  • Interpretable intelligent systems for real-world deployment
  • Multimodal learning, edge AI, and real-time inference
  • AI for social good, public policy, and digital governance
  • Neuromorphic computing, brain-inspired models
  • Fairness, robustness, and reproducibility in industrial AI
  • Other cross-disciplinary applications of AI in industry

Proposal Guidelines

Each Special Session proposal should include:

  • Title of the Session
  • Names, affiliations, and short bios of the organizers
  • Session abstract (max 300 words) describing the focus and relevance
  • List of potential topics to be covered
  • Tentative list of invited speakers or potential contributors (if any)
  • Plan for promoting the session and attracting submissions
