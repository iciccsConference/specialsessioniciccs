Session Abstract

This special session on AI for Industry focuses on innovative AI applications in agriculture and industrial domains, with an emphasis on plant disease detection, sustainable farming practices, and explainable AI solutions. The session will highlight the role of large language models (LLMs) and interpretable AI techniques to provide actionable insights for farmers, agronomists, and industry practitioners. Participants will discuss cutting-edge approaches for multimodal learning, real-time monitoring, and AI-driven decision support systems, fostering collaboration between AI researchers and industrial stakeholders.

Topics of Interest

  • AI-driven plant disease detection and precision agriculture
  • Explainable and interpretable AI systems for industry applications
  • Large language models (LLMs) for industrial knowledge extraction
  • Multimodal AI for crop monitoring and yield prediction
  • Sustainable and smart farming solutions using AI
  • Robustness, fairness, and reproducibility in industrial AI applications
  • Edge AI and real-time inference for industrial processes
  • AI for social good and sustainable development in agriculture
  • Integration of IoT and AI for smart farming

Promotion Plan

The session will be promoted via IEEE networks, academic mailing lists, social media, and targeted invitations to researchers and industry practitioners in AI and agriculture. We will actively encourage high-quality submissions to ensure a dynamic and engaging session.

Call for Papers

We invite researchers, practitioners, and students to submit original contributions on the application of AI in industry, with a focus on agriculture and plant disease detection. Submissions should highlight novel techniques, practical implementations, or interdisciplinary approaches combining AI, LLMs, and explainable systems. All papers will be peer-reviewed, and accepted papers will be included in the ICICCS 2026 proceedings.
