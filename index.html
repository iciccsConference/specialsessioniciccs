About the Special Session

The “AI in Industry” special session at ICICCS Conference invites researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to share advances and applications of Artificial Intelligence across diverse sectors. This session aims to bridge the gap between academic research and real-world industrial implementation of AI.

Call for Papers

We invite original research, review papers, and industrial case studies related to the application of AI in industry. Topics include, but are not limited to:

Session Topics

  • AI for Manufacturing and Automation
  • Industrial IoT & Predictive Maintenance
  • AI in Supply Chain & Logistics
  • Robotics and Machine Vision
  • AI for Smart Energy Management
  • AI-driven Decision Support Systems
  • Quality Control using AI
  • AI in Healthcare Industry
  • Natural Language Processing for Industry
  • Security, Ethics, and Standards in Industrial AI
